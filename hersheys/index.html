We assume the $40,000 monthly volume is primarily card-present transactions typical for table-service restaurants. For calculations, we use an estimated blended interchange rate of 2.0% (industry average for restaurants, which may vary based on card mix).

Option 1: Interchange + 0.45% markup on all transactions.

Both options eliminate junk fees like monthly, PCI, AVS, batch, and network fees, aligning with Xplor Technologies' transparent pricing.

This straightforward pricing applies uniformly across all channels via XplorPay's integrated platform.

OPTION 2: 3% Surcharge on All Credit Cards

Program Details

XplorPay's compliant surcharging program adds a 3% fee transparently at checkout for credit card payments (debit cards exempt per regulations). This is automated through our POS integrations, with no manual adjustments needed. Customers see the fee clearly, and you receive the surcharge revenue to offset processing costs.

  • Surcharge: 3% on credit card volume
  • Processor Markup: None
  • Monthly Fees: None
  • Other Junk Fees: None

Assumes 70% credit card volume ($28,000) and 30% debit ($12,000) based on industry averages. Interchange estimated at 2.2% for credit (~$616) and 1.2% for debit (~$144).

Estimated Financials Under Option 2

  • Surcharge Revenue (Credit): 3% × $28,000 = $840.00
  • Total Interchange: $760.00
  • Net Position: $840.00 (revenue) – $760.00 (interchange) = +$80.00 profit

Effective Cost: Net profit of $80/month (instead of paying fees).

Pros and Cons of Option 2

Pros Cons
  • Turns processing into a profit center with net positive returns.
  • No processor markup—surcharge offsets costs.
  • Fully compliant and automated via XplorPay's POS partnerships (e.g., HYFIN, KWIKPOS).
  • Eliminates junk fees; encourages debit or cash use.
  • Scalable for growing volume.
  • Visible surcharge on credit cards may require guest communication (templates provided).
  • Debit transactions still incur interchange (not surcharged).
  • Requires compliant signage and disclosure.

Option 2 – Executive Summary

With a 3% surcharge on credit cards, Hershey's offsets interchange costs and generates net profit. XplorPay's integrated solutions ensure compliance and smooth operations without impacting your table-service flow.
