The Challenge

Modern constituents and customers expect seamless digital payment experiences, yet many organizations still rely on manual processes, phone payments, or disconnected third-party portals. Payment integration requires careful attention to security standards (PCI DSS), fraud prevention, reconciliation, and user experience—all while managing transaction costs and compliance risk.

Our Approach

Payment Industry Expertise

Deep knowledge of payment processing, compliance requirements (PCI DSS, AML), and merchant services enables secure, cost-effective implementations leveraging proven payment technologies.

Integrated User Experience

Payment flows are embedded directly into your applications and portals rather than redirecting to external sites, maintaining brand consistency and user trust.

Enterprise Payment Solutions

Leverage proven payment technologies and processing relationships to deliver reliable, scalable payment capabilities with favorable economics.

Capabilities

  • Payment Gateway Integration: Credit card, ACH, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and alternative payment methods
  • Citizen Payment Portals: Self-service payment for taxes, permits, fines, utility bills, and service fees with receipt generation and history
  • Subscription & Recurring Billing: Automated recurring charges, subscription management, and usage-based billing for SaaS products
  • Invoice & AR Automation: Automated invoice generation, payment reminders, and accounts receivable reconciliation
  • PCI Compliance: Tokenization, secure payment forms, and compliance framework implementation to minimize PCI scope
  • Fraud Prevention: Transaction monitoring, velocity checks, AVS/CVV verification, and chargeback management
  • Multi-Tenant Payment Management: Isolated payment processing for SaaS platforms serving multiple organizations or departments
  • Reporting & Reconciliation: Real-time transaction dashboards, settlement reporting, and automated reconciliation with accounting systems
