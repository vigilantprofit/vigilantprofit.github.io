About Vigilant Profit Solutions

Founder-led technology consulting that delivers practical results for public sector and enterprise clients.

Our Mission

Vigilant Profit Solutions exists to help organizations bridge the gap between modern technology capabilities and real operational needs. We believe technology should serve mission outcomes—not the other way around—and that successful implementations require both technical excellence and deep understanding of business context.

Founder-Led Engagement Model

Our engagements are led by a founder who combines:

  • Account Executive Experience: Deep understanding of client needs, stakeholder management, and value communication from years in consultative sales and advisory roles
  • Payment Industry Background: Hands-on knowledge of payment processing, compliance (PCI DSS, AML), and financial technology from direct experience in the payments sector
  • Technical Fluency: Working understanding of modern development practices, cloud architecture, and AI implementation—not just conceptual knowledge

This combination means you work with someone who can translate between business requirements and technical delivery, maintain accountability throughout the engagement, and ensure solutions align to measurable outcomes rather than technology trends.

Partner Ecosystem

Through established master service agreements with specialized engineering, product development, and IT service firms, we can scale delivery capacity while maintaining single-point accountability. This model allows us to:

  • Match team size and skill mix to project requirements without overhead of large permanent staff
  • Access deep technical expertise in niche areas (AI/ML, mobile development, DevOps, cybersecurity)
  • Deliver both targeted advisory work and larger implementation programs
  • Maintain quality and consistency through direct oversight and standardized delivery practices

Industries & Clients

We serve public sector organizations (municipal, county, state, and federal agencies) and mid-market enterprises that need enterprise-grade capabilities without enterprise-scale overhead. Our experience spans:

  • Government services and citizen engagement
  • Financial services and payments
  • Healthcare and human services
  • Professional services and business consulting
  • Technology and SaaS companies
