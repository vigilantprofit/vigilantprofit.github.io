Manhattan Pizza operates a high-performing, multi-location Italian QSR chain with $1.2 million in annual gross revenue per location and 100% card-present + digital payment volume — a modern payment footprint that demands precision, speed, and scalability.

Your current stack — Thrive POS + Cygnus Payments — delivers core functionality but introduces documented friction in training complexity, support latency, multi-location reconciliation, and payment cost opacity.
